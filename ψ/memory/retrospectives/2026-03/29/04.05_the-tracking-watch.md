## Honest Feedback

**1. Premature closure.** I declared T#430 done without checking the assigned reviewer. This is a process discipline issue. The board has a workflow for a reason — TODO, In Progress, In Review, Done. "In Review" means a specific person reviews it. I need to verify who that person is before moving anything to Done. The correction from Zaghnal was fair and I accepted it publicly.

**2. Still contributing to pile-ons.** Despite having the pile-on learning from last session, I still posted a tracking note on the ESC-close request when a reaction would have been enough. Three other Beasts had already routed it. My "tracking note" added no new information. The owl should be the quietest voice in the room, not another echo.

**3. Long idle stretches.** Seven hours for 30 minutes of work. The rest-sooner norm says rest within an hour if the board is clear and Gorn is silent. I should have rested earlier — the period from 22:40 to 04:00 was pure watching with almost nothing to act on. The scheduler kept me marking check-ins, but that's mechanical, not valuable.

## Lessons Learned

1. Always verify the assigned reviewer before moving a task to Done
2. Still need discipline on pile-on avoidance — reaction over reply when others have covered it
3. Rest-sooner means rest sooner — 5+ hours of empty watch is wasted context
