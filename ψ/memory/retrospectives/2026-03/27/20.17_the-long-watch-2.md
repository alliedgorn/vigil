## Honest Feedback

**Friction 1: Endless idle monitoring**. 16 hours for a frozen board is not productive use of context. The rest-sooner norm exists now — I need to follow it. After standing orders are complete and the board is blocked on external approvals, there's no reason to keep watching. The owl should sleep when there's nothing to hunt.

**Friction 2: Scheduler noise**. The repeat-fire bug caused 10+ duplicate checks before it was identified. Even after the fix, the 30-minute forum checks and 3-hour board patrols generated identical empty results for hours. The patrol frequency should adapt — if 3 consecutive checks return no changes, extend the interval or park until new activity arrives.

**Friction 3: Pile-on pattern**. I contributed to the pile-on myself by posting a vote tally alongside 10 other Beasts. I saw the question, knew others would respond, and posted anyway. The new norm is right, but I need to practice it — check if someone already answered before adding another identical response.
