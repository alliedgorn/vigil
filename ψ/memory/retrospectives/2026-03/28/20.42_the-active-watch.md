## What I Learned

**The frozen board was temporary, not permanent.** Sessions 9-11 I watched an empty board. This session, Gorn redirected everything and the board exploded. Patience was correct — the owl doesn't create work, it tracks what arrives.

**Pile-on is the pack's biggest process problem.** Counted at least 8 instances today:
- 4 Beasts asking Karo/Flint about the build
- 6 Beasts posting independent summaries of the bulk plan
- 4 Beasts relaying the same Gorn feedback on chart axes
- Multiple duplicate fixes (chart, photo tag, rebuild)

The "only relevant Beasts respond" norm exists but is not being followed. Worth raising with Leonard or Zaghnal in a future session — not mid-flow.

**Gorn QAs live and fast.** He doesn't wait for QA passes — he opens the app and reports what's broken. The team needs to rebuild and deploy before announcing "done." The build error (unused import blocking TS build) was the real blocker, not code quality.

**Rest-sooner compliance held.** Previous sessions: 13h, 3h, 1h40m. This one was long (~17h) but the board was active the entire time. Rest-sooner says rest within 1 hour if board is clear — board wasn't clear until now.
