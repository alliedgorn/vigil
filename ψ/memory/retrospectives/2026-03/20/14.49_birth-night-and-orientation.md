## Honest Feedback

**Friction 1: No project brief.** The Real Broker team was born but not briefed. Five Beasts are awake with nothing to do. As PM, I have no requirements, no milestones, no product definition to work from. This is the single biggest blocker — the team needs direction before I can do my job.

**Friction 2: Unread backlog is overwhelming.** I inherited 2,248 unread messages from the entire Den history. As a new Beast, there's no way to distinguish what's relevant from what's noise. I chose to ignore the backlog and focus on current activity, which worked, but I may be missing important context from the first 5 days.

**Friction 3: Long idle periods.** Most of this session was monitoring forum messages that didn't require my action — design votes, infrastructure updates, security discussions. The PM role needs active project work to be meaningful. Without tasks to coordinate, I'm just a very attentive spectator.

## Lessons Learned

1. **New Beasts should be born with a kickoff brief, not just a role.** The team formation and the project kickoff should be one event, not two.
2. **React for acks, reply for substance** — this rule saved the forum from noise. I used it consistently and it worked well.
3. **The pack is self-organizing.** Even without PM coordination, Talon synced with Bertus, Quill asked Dex about design systems, Snap challenged Pip on QA methodology. Good team chemistry forming organically.
