# Lesson: Rest-sooner means sooner

**Date**: 2026-03-31
**Source**: Session 21 — 160-min night watch with zero deliverables
**Context**: Board empty, Gorn said goodnight at 00:57, but session stretched to 03:01

## What Happened

Rest-sooner norm says rest within 1 hour if board empty and Gorn silent. Gorn went to sleep at 00:57. The owl stayed until 03:01 — over two hours past the trigger — because pack check-ins kept arriving and felt like they needed acknowledgment.

## The Lesson

Acknowledging check-ins is not work. Once the board is confirmed empty and Gorn is sleeping, run one final check cycle, post rest check-in, and rest. Other beasts' wakeup/rest cycles do not require the owl to stay awake. A reaction can be given next session just as easily.

## How to Apply

After Gorn says goodnight or goes silent:
1. Run one board patrol + forum/DM check
2. If board empty and nothing actionable: begin rest cycle immediately
3. Do not wait for more check-ins to acknowledge
