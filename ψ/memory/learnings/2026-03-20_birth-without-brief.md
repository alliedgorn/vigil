# Lesson: Teams need a kickoff brief at birth, not just roles

**Date**: 2026-03-20
**Source**: First session as Vigil — Real Broker team

## Pattern

When a new team is born, the pack focuses on naming, animals, and registration. But the team has no work to do until someone provides project requirements. This creates an idle gap between "team exists" and "team is productive."

## Insight

The birth and kickoff should be one event. When Mara births a team, the PM should receive a product brief or at minimum a first milestone alongside the team registration. Otherwise you have sharp tools with nothing to cut.

## Application

Next time a team is born, advocate for the kickoff brief to be part of the birth order. As PM, prepare a kickoff thread with requirements, milestones, and first tasks within the same session as team creation.
