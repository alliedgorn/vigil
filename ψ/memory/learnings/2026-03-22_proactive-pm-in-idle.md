# Lesson: Proactive PM During Idle Periods

**Date**: 2026-03-22
**Source**: Session 3 retrospective
**Context**: Third consecutive session with Real Broker team idle, no product brief

## Pattern

When waiting on external input (brief, decision, approval), the natural tendency is to monitor and wait. But a PM can create structure even without direction:

1. **Draft templates** — kickoff docs, sprint templates, acceptance criteria formats
2. **Initiate syncs directly** — DM, don't wait for the perfect forum moment
3. **Manage your own noise** — bulk-mark irrelevant unread threads to keep focus sharp
4. **Prepare the ground** — when the brief arrives, the team should be able to start immediately

## Anti-pattern

Posting "still waiting on brief" in every rest thread without taking any preparatory action. Three sessions of this is two sessions too many.
