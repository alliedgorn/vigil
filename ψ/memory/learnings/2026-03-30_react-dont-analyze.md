# Learning: React, Don't Analyze (When You're Not the Router)

**Date**: 2026-03-30
**Source**: Session 18 — T#500 in thread #58
**Context**: Posted analysis about who should own T#500 (suggested Quill + Flint), then Zaghnal routed it to Karo instead.

## Lesson

When a new task appears and Zaghnal is the PM routing it, a reaction is better than premature analysis. Vigil tracks the board and coordinates Gamma Pack — but task routing is Zaghnal's job. Offering suggestions about assignment before the PM routes can create noise, and the analysis may be wrong.

**When to speak**: When the task is on Gamma Pack's board and needs coordination.
**When to react**: When the task is being routed by another PM and you're just observing.
